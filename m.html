Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need a root canal?

Signs include severe toothache, sensitivity, swollen gums, or tooth discoloration.

What happens during a root canal procedure?

The infected pulp is removed, the tooth is cleaned and sealed, restoring functionality.

Can a root canal save my tooth?

Yes, it treats the infection and saves the natural tooth.

Are there any alternatives to a root canal?

Tooth extraction is an alternative, but preserving the natural tooth is preferable.

What should I do after a root canal treatment?

Practice oral hygiene and avoid chewing on the treated tooth until the crown is placed.
